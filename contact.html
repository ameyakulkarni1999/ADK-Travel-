Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for international trips, especially during peak seasons. However, we can also help with last-minute bookings based on availability.

Do you handle visa applications?

Yes! We provide guidance on visa requirements and can assist with the application process for most destinations. We'll ensure you have all necessary documentation.

What's included in your travel packages?

Our packages typically include accommodations, transportation, guided tours, and some meals. Each package is customized based on your preferences and budget.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Absolutely! We strongly recommend travel insurance and can help you choose the right coverage for your trip, including medical, cancellation, and baggage protection.
